WikiAR

When touristing in foreign countries the language barrier is often a problem. Finding your hotel or traveling can be difficult and time consuming as well as finding information when sightseeing. To be able to make the most of your travels we give you the consept of WikiAR. WikiAR gives you real time information about travel alternatives, landmarks and guides you to your destination.

VindrutAR

VindrutAR is the concept of having an AR-solution for the windshield of a car. Not only your car, but every car sold. This would include a speedometer and fuel-indicator but would also have options connected to your GPS, speedlimits, road traction and vehicle tracking for safety on the road. Everything so that you never have to take your eyes of the road.

LagAR

We believe that the world could use more repurposed items. From clothing and textiles to bikes and furniture - why not repupose? The concept of LagAR helps you mend and fix what can be re-used, which is good for the environment but also your wallet. With the help of tutorial videos and AI solutions for measuring and identifying objects the handy man’s world will be everyone’s world!
